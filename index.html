What is Exercise?

Exercise is any physical activity that helps improve your body’s fitness, strength, and overall health. It is an important part of a healthy lifestyle because it keeps your heart, muscles, and mind strong.

Why Exercise is Important

Regular exercise helps reduce stress, improves mood, boosts energy levels, and lowers the risk of diseases such as heart disease and diabetes. It also improves sleep quality and helps you maintain a healthy weight.

Types of Exercise

Aerobic: Running, cycling, swimming, walking

Strength Training: Weight lifting, push-ups, squats

Flexibility: Stretching and yoga movements

Balance: Yoga and tai chi exercises
