What is exercise?

Exercise is a physical activity that involves repetitive movements to improve or maintain physical fitness.


Top 10 benefits of exercise


Types of Exercises

Aerobic - Also known as cardio, these exercises strengthen your heart and lungs and increase your endurance. Examples include running, biking, and brisk walking.

Strength - Also known as resistance training, these exercises help tone and strengthen your muscles and bones. Examples include lifting weights, push-ups, lunges, and squats.

Stretching - These exercises help you move your joints and muscles through their full range of motion, making you more flexible. Stretching can be done every day.

Balance - These exercises help you stay upright and oriented, and can help prevent falls. Examples include yoga and tai chi.

Compound movements - These exercises use multiple muscle groups. Examples include the squat, bench press, deadlift, and overhead press.
